Jason Barrera

I have been part of the lab since Spring 2023 and am currently majoring in geology. During this time, I have contributed to multiple research projects, focusing on the collection and analysis of amino acids across a diverse range of marine life, from phytoplankton to sharks and tuna. My research has taken me to incredible places, including a 10-day research cruise off the coast of California, where I collected zooplankton samples. Beyond my research, I also take on the task of mentoring new undergraduates, teaching them laboratory methods and experimental techniques.
